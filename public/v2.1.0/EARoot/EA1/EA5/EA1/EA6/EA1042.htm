The Control profiles for devices which provide energy capacity to the grid (Generation, ESS, inverters, cap controllers, etc..), and are being operated in a grid-following mode, coordinated by a circuit-segment-service acting as a distributed energy management scheduler. When in Grid Forming mode, use the associated DiscreteControlProfile<br/><br/>
